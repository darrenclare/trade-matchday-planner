How this works

Group Stage is the group stage: enter scores and tables update. Knockout Stage is the knockout stage: send qualifiers across, tap winners through, crown a champion. Use Mate League and Site Banter to make it shareable.

Group Stage

Four teams per group. Everyone plays everyone once. Win = 3 points, draw = 1, loss = 0. Top two go through, plus the eight best third-place teams.

How to use it

Enter scores as games happen or make predictions before kick-off. The group tables update automatically. The Third-Place Scrap shows which third-place teams are clinging on.

Third-Place Scrap

Squeaky-bum time. Top 8 survive, bottom 4 are off home.

Knockout Stage

Lose and you’re out. Fill it from the group tables, tweak any slot if needed, then tap teams through to the final.

How to use it

Use “Fill from Group Stage” after entering scores. Knockout route slots stay TBC until the groups give us qualifiers. Tap a team to advance them. If third-place routes are awkward, the app makes a sensible pick and lets you adjust.
